About Joke Generator

Welcome to the Joke Generator! This web page is a simple application that provides you with a random joke every time you click the "Get Another Joke" button. It's designed to bring laughter and joy to your day.

How it Works

The Joke Generator fetches random jokes from the Official Joke API. When you click the "Get Another Joke" button, the page makes a request to the API, receives a random joke, and displays it on the page.
